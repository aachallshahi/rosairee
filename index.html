FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Ribbon bouquets are handcrafted from premium satin ribbon — each petal, leaf, and stem is shaped by hand. Unlike real flowers, they never wilt, making them a lasting keepsake. They look strikingly similar to fresh flowers and can be customised in any color.

The price is the sum of: (number of each flower × flower price) + accessories. For example, butterfly pins are Rs 30 each, leaves are Rs 10 each, and snow paper is charged per meter based on the paper rate at the time of order.

Absolutely! When placing your order, mention your preferred colors in the special instructions. We have a wide range of ribbon shades — from pastels to deep jewel tones. Subject to availability, we'll accommodate your preference.

Standard orders take 2–4 business days. For large or complex bouquets (20+ flowers, special accessories), allow 5–7 days. We recommend ordering at least a week before your event for best results.

Yes! We offer local delivery within the city. Delivery charges vary by distance and will be communicated when you place your order. We also offer pickup from our studio.

Snow paper is priced per meter used and rates may vary depending on the type of snow paper chosen (plain, printed, glitter, etc.). Our team will inform you of the exact rate when you confirm your order. In the builder, enter the price per meter and the quantity you wish to use.
