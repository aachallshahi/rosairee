Frequently Asked Questions

Ribbon bouquets are handcrafted from premium satin ribbon — each petal, leaf, and stem shaped by hand. Unlike real flowers, they never wilt, making them a lasting keepsake. They look strikingly similar to fresh flowers and can be customised in any color.

The price is the sum of all selected flowers + accessories. Butterfly pins: Rs 30 each. Leaves: Rs 10 each. Polaroid photo: Rs 30 each. Plushie: Rs 500 each. Wrapping paper: Rs 25/sheet (auto-estimated by bouquet size). Snow paper: Rs 100/meter. Chocolates range from Rs 50–Rs 220 per piece depending on type.

Absolutely — and it's important! All flowers are available in any ribbon color. When placing your order, mention your preferred colors in the color section or special instructions. We have a wide range of satin ribbon shades from pastels to deep jewel tones. Subject to availability, we'll accommodate your preference.

Standard orders take 2–4 business days. For large or complex bouquets (20+ flowers, special accessories), allow 5–7 days. We recommend ordering at least a week before your event for best results.

Yes! We offer local delivery within the city. Delivery charges vary by distance and will be communicated when you place your order. We also offer pickup from our studio.

Snow paper wrapping is priced by bouquet size — Rs 50 for small bouquets (1–7 flowers) and Rs 100 for large bouquets (8+ flowers). The cost is automatically added to your total when you select "Yes" for snow paper in the builder.
